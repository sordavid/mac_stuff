How to Make Tomato Soup

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Chicken Broth
  • *Strainer

    • Note: * indicates that it is optional

  1. Boil Tomatoes, onion, garlic cloves, and broth into a pot until the flavors blend
  2. Strain the boiled ingredients from the pot into a large bowl to seperate chunks. (Soup should be as smooth as possible)
  3. Done! Serve to guests with bread.
